Web-based Barangay Management System

About us

Barangay Management System is capable of capturing large amounts of data and storing it for an extended length of time. This solution can help accomplish strategic goals such as lowering paper usage and time-consuming file retrieval from bulky documents. BMS can keep track of residents' records, such as personal and family information, complaints and peaceful settlements, and can provide daily reports for the Barangay.
