Web-based Barangay Transaction Management System

About the system

This is a Web-based Transaction Management System for Barangay Santol QC. It is capable of performing paperless transactions that help increase the efficiency and productivity of barangay employees and residents. Its E-blotter feature allows residents to submit a blotter request within seconds and it being on the Internet means that users and employeees can access the system anywhere, anytime for as long as they have an Internet connection.
